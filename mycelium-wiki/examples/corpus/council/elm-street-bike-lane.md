# Resolution 2026-14 — Elm Street Bike Lane

On 2026-03-10 the council approved Resolution 2026-14, installing a protected
bike lane on Elm Street between 1st Avenue and 5th Avenue. Funding is £120,000
from the active-travel budget. The vote was 7 in favour, 2 against. Construction
is scheduled to begin 2026-06-01 and finish before 2026-09-30.
