# Resolution 2026-18 — Bus Route 12 Extension

On 2026-04-14 the council approved Resolution 2026-18, extending Bus Route 12 to
the new Riverside estate. Buses run every 30 minutes on weekdays and hourly at
weekends, starting 2026-07-01. Funding is £85,000 per year from the transport
budget. The vote was 8 in favour, 1 against. Patronage is reviewed after twelve
months.
