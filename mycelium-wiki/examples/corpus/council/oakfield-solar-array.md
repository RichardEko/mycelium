# Resolution 2026-21 — Oakfield Solar Array

On 2026-05-06 the council approved Resolution 2026-21, installing a 150 kW
community solar array on the Oakfield leisure-centre roof. Capital cost is
£165,000 from the climate reserve, with an expected annual saving of £22,000 on
the centre's electricity bill. The vote was 9 in favour, 0 against.
Commissioning is scheduled for 2026-10-01.
