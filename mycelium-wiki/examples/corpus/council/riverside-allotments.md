# Resolution 2026-24 — Riverside Allotments

On 2026-05-19 the council approved Resolution 2026-24, creating 40 new allotment
plots on the old Riverside depot land. Groundworks — clearance, water supply, and
fencing — cost £52,000 from the capital budget. Plots let at £35 per year, with
priority given to households on the existing waiting list. First lettings begin
2026-09-01.
